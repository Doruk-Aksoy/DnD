- Ramping Assault (3)
	* Every 6/5/4th melee hit on the same enemy deals 20% more damage.
	
- Cranium Bash (3)
	* Blunt melee weapons have 3/6/9% chance to stun enemies for 1.5 seconds.

- Echoing Strikes (2) [5]
	* Melee attacks have 12.5% chance to splash around for 50% of the damage.
	+ 12.5% chance.
	- Brutal Strikes (1) or Cranium Bash (1)

- Unending Fury (2) [10]
	* Berserk's damage bonus applies to all weapons at 50% of its value.
	+ 25% additional factor.
	- Requires Ramping Assault.
	
- Fervent Reach (2) [12]
	* Melee splash effects can occur 20% further.
	+ 20% additional splash distance.
	- Requires Echoing Strikes.

- Exhauster (3)
	* Melee hits have 10% chance to slow enemies hit by 20%.
	+ +5% chance to slow.

- Deep Cuts (3)
	* Slashing melee hits have +15% chance to inflict bleeding on hit.
	+ 7.5% bleeding chance.
	
- Quick & Deadly (1) [6]
	* Slashing attacks repeat an additional time and are 33% faster.

- Swift & Precise (1) [8]
	* Melee hits grant 10% movement speed per enemy hit up to 100% for 3 seconds.

===============================

- Flash Parry (3)
	* Parrying projectiles releases a shockwave forward for a limited distance, dealing 100 + 5% max health damage to enemies.
	+ 50 base damage and 10% shockwave travel distance.

- Riposte (1) [3]
	* Successful parries reduce subsequent parry costs by 10% up to a maximum of 30% within a 10 second window.

- Martial Finesse (1) [12]
	* Melee hits reduce melee cooldowns by 10%.
